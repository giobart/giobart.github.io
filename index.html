Giovanni Bartolomeo is a researcher at the Chair of Connected Mobility of the Technical University of Munich, Germany. He has a Master’s cum laude in Computer Science (2021) from the University of Pisa, Italy. His main research subject is edge and cloud computing, focusing on service orchestration and networking solutions for heterogeneous edge infrastructures.
